# Bias(ed) Maps

**DOI**: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.16943470

During the workshops held for the Combatting Bias project, we posed the question 'What is bias?'. Through collaborative collaging - a conscious and creative act to make complex concepts concrete - the following bias(ed) maps were created. 

We are immensely grateful for all those who have contributed their time, knowledge and skill to these wonderful maps.
